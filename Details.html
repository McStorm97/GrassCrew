A range of services to suit every property and every yard maintenance need

Lawn Mowing

This covers a basic lawn mow just to keep your lawn trim, this can include leaving clippings on site or taking away depending on your budget and needs.

Edge and Line Trimming

After the mow we go around and make sure all of your edging is trimmed nicely as required for your lawn.

Brooming, Raking and Tidying

During autumn in particular you may get a lot of leaf fall. We can keep your yard tidy by brooming off paths and driveways, raking up leaves and taking it all away.

Gardening

We can also keep your gardens tidy. we offer weeding, plant trimming and hedge trimming.

Large Tree Trimming

We can organise an arborist and oversee the work for you if you have large trees or hedges that need hacking back.

Organic Disposal


Have you done a big tidy up yourself and have no idea what to do with the organic matter? Give us a call, and we can come collect it and dispose of it for you.
